Predicting and Explaining Electoral Support for the Far-Right in the Netherlands: From Boomers to Zoomers

MSc thesis using machine learning to predict far-right voting in the Netherlands. Compared LightGBM, Random Forest, and Logistic Regression, achieving 93% accuracy and AUC 0.97.

Using SHAP, I unpacked why people vote this way. In 2010, immigration attitudes were the dominant predictor; by 2023, leader sympathy and party identity had taken over. Data showed Gen Z is not more extreme than other generations, while Millennials showed the widest political range.
